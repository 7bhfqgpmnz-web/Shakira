Albums

Signature albums that shaped Shakira’s sound and global career.

Laundry Service

2001 · Studio Album

The breakthrough English-language album that introduced Shakira to global audiences. Featuring pop-rock energy, Middle Eastern elements and worldwide hits like “Whenever, Wherever,” it remains one of her most influential records.

Fijación Oral, Vol. 1

2005 · Studio Album

A critically acclaimed Spanish-language project showcasing Shakira’s vocal depth, poetic lyricism and orchestral pop arrangements. Widely regarded as one of her most artistically rich albums.

El Dorado

2017 · Studio Album

A vibrant Latin pop album blending reggaeton, dance rhythms and emotional ballads. The project earned multiple awards and solidified Shakira’s influence in modern Latin music.
